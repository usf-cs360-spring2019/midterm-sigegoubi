Project Description

With assigned SF Fire Department Call for Services dataset Our team decide to visualize the efficiency of SF Fire Department among January, 2019. The visualizations are mostly focus on the response time of SF Fire Department versus different aspects of variables such as location, priority, seasons, etc.
